AI Benchmark Index

Tracking model performance across reasoning, coding, math, and multimodal tasks for frontier and open-source models. Updated weekly.

What This Index Covers

Reasoning Benchmarks

MMLU, ARC, HellaSwag, and other reasoning evaluations tracking logical and commonsense capabilities.

Coding Benchmarks

HumanEval, MBPP, SWE-Bench, and code generation evaluations across multiple programming languages.

Math Benchmarks

GSM8K, MATH, and advanced mathematical reasoning assessments for quantitative problem solving.

Multimodal Benchmarks

Vision-language tasks, image understanding, and cross-modal reasoning evaluations for multimodal models.

Methodology

Benchmark data is collected from official leaderboards, published papers, model cards, and reproducible evaluation suites. Scores are normalized and tracked over time for trend analysis.
